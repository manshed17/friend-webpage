Historical Places

Emperors Palace



The place is also known as the palace of dreams. It is situated next to OR Tambo International airport. A place where where one can rest their heads. It offers a decadent accommodation in four unique hotels, a glorious health and beauty spa, a magnificent casino with unparalleled gaming enjoyment, the finest dining options (Rosettas being my favourite and highly recomment it), spectacular entertainment choices, world class conference facilities, and impeccable service.


JHB ZOO-LAKE
Fun and leisure. It is situated in Saxonwold next to Johannesburg Zoo, which forms part of the greater Zoo Lake area. Zoo Lake is a popular spot for picnicking, taking a leisurely boat ride. It has its own information centre and has one of the popular African themed restaurants, Moyo. The Zoo Lake Sports Club is another favourite with residents, offering a variety of sports amenities, a restaurant and pub.





Gold Reef City



Fun and games. A family friendly theme park. Gold Reef City is a premier entertainment destination situated in the south of Johannesburg. It offers a variety of entertainment which includes a thrilling theme park, a spectacular theatre featuring live productions and a variety of restaurants and fast foods for every taste.
